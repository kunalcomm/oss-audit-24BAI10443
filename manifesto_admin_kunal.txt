Open Source Manifesto — admin_kunal — 31 March 2026

I believe in the power of open source. Every day I use Python,
a tool built freely by people who chose to share their work with the world.
To me, freedom means choice — and that is exactly what open source represents.
One day I will build a learning app and share it freely, because knowledge grows
when it is given away. Standing on the shoulders of giants, I choose
to be a giant for those who come after me.
